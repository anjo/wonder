If you want to request dates for a different year, choose the year (at the top of the screen).

Dates are inclusive. If you want to book 1 day off then enter it as:
From TO
For half days, choose AM or PM to indicate the period you're off. You should not include holidays or weekends as part of the total.
Minimum request is 0.5 days.

If you have a holiday that spans over two years, you need to split it into two parts; with the TO, FROM dates inclusive within one year. 

When you've completed a request, click the REQUEST button.
